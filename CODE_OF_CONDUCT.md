# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Give and receive feedback in good faith
- Focus criticism on ideas, not people
- Assume positive intent while seeking clarity

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Personal attacks or derogatory language
- Publishing private information without consent
- Disruptive trolling or repeated bad-faith behavior

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct and may remove or restrict contributions that violate these standards.

## Reporting

To report unacceptable behavior, open a private discussion with maintainers via the repository contact channels.
